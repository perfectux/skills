# Industry Anchors

Use only when a UX decision needs standards grounding. Do not turn this skill into a standards report.

## Practical Mapping

- WCAG 2.2: accessibility baseline for perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust experiences.
- Nielsen Norman Group heuristics: status visibility, user control, consistency, error prevention, recognition over recall, recovery, and help.
- ISO 9241-110:2020: task fit, self-descriptive interaction, expectation fit, learnability, controllability, error robustness, and engagement.
- GOV.UK Service Standard: understand users, solve the whole service problem, keep flows simple, accessible, measurable, secure, and iterated.
- Microsoft Inclusive Design: recognize exclusion and design for real human diversity.
- Microsoft HAX Toolkit: set AI expectations, show feedback, support correction, and design for changing AI behavior over time.
- NIST AI RMF: consider AI trust, safety, privacy, security, and downstream risk.
